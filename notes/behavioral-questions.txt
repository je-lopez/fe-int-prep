1. Tell me about a time when you were faced with a problem that had a number of  possible solutions.
  - What was the problem and how did you determine the course of action?
  - What was the outcome of that choice?

2. When did you take a risk, make a mistake, or fail?
  - How did you respond, and how did you grow from that experience?

3. Describe a time you took the lead on a project.

4. How have you leveraged data to develop a strategy?

5. What interest you about the role you're interviewing for and team?
